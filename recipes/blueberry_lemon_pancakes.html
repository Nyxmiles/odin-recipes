Blueberry Lemon Pancakes

Preparation:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in large mixing bowl
  2. Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients
  3. Pour onto greased sheetpan
  4. Make crumble with brown sugar, flour and butter
  5. Bake at 400F for 22 to 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 380g Oat flour
  • 2tbsp Baking powder
  • 4 Scoops vanilla protein powder
  • 4tbsp Sweetner
  • 650g Cottage cheese
  • 170g Egg whites
  • 250ml whole milk
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 6oz Blueberries
  • 1tsp Vanilla extract
