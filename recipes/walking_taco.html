Walking Taco

Preparation:

  1. Cook ground beef until browned using taco seasoining
  2. Put beef in bag of chips
  3. Add toppings as desired

Ingredients:

  • 8oz Lean ground beef
  • 1 Serving cheddar cheese
  • 1 bag Quest protein chips
  • 1/4cup Greek yogurt
  • Lettuce
