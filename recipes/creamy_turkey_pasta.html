Creamy Turkey Pasta

Preparation:

  1. Cook turkey until brown with italian seasoning, garlic and onions
  2. Add all wet ingredients and combine
  3. Cook pasta on side. When finished, add to pan with turkey and mix.

Ingredients:

  • 100g Yellow onion
  • 5g Garlic
  • 1tbsp Light butter
  • 756g Lean ground turkey
  • 645g Pasta sauce
  • 85g Light cream cheese
  • 225g Fat free Greek yogurt
  • 340g Protein pasta
  • 3tbsp Parmesean cheese
