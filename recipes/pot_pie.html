Pot Pie

Preparation:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Mix all ingredients and place into baking dish
  3. Bake for 30-40min
  4. Place crescent roll atop the pie filling, and bake until golden brown

Ingredients:

  • 28oz Canned chicken breast
  • 720g frozen peas and carrots
  • A medium onion, diced
  • 290g Blended cottage cheese
  • 180g Non-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1cup Chicken bone broth
  • 24g Cornstarch
  • 1 1/2tbsp Minced garlic
  • 1 can reduced fat crescent roll
