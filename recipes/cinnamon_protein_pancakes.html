Cinnamon Pancakes

Preparation:

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, protein powder, sweetner, cinnamon, cottage cheese, egg whites and milk
  2. Pour mixture onto sheetpan
  3. Mix brown sweetner, maple syrup, cinnamon, and add spoonfulls onto the batter, spreading out into a pattern
  4. Bake at 425F for 15-20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4cups Oat flour
  • 2tbsp Baking powder
  • 4 Scoops vanilla protein powder
  • 3tbsp Cinnamon
  • 720g Cottage cheese
  • 180g Egg whites
  • 1 1/3cup Fat free milk
  • 1/3cup brown sugar
  • 4tbsp Maple syrup
