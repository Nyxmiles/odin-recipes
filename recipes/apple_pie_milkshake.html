Apple Pie Milkshake

Preparation:

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until smooth

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of ice
  • 240ml Skim milk
  • 130g Apple pie filling
  • 55g Vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1 Scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1tbsp 0 Calorie sweetner
  • 1/4tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/4tsp Ginger
  • 1/4tsp Nutmeg
