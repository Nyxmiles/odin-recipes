No-Bake Strawberry Cheesecake

Preparation:

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Layer slices of strawberries on bottom of ramekin
  3. Pour mixture atop strawberries
  4. Top with crumbles of crackers
  5. Freeze for at least 10min

Ingredients:

  • 1cup Fat free vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 1/4cup Low fat cream cheese
  • 1scoop Vanilla protien powder
  • 2tbsp powdered sweetener
  • 1 1/2tbsp cheesecake sugar-free pudding mix
  • 1/2tsp vanilla extract
  • 2tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2cup strawberries
  • Crushed graham crackers
