Chili Lime Burrito

Preparation:

  1. Dice chicken breast
  2. Coat with olive oil, salt, pepper, chili powder, cumin and lime juice
  3. Cook on stove, or in air fryer until well done
  4. Mix Greek yogurt and hot sauce of choice to make sauce
  5. Layer sauce, chicken, onions, lettuce and tomatoes on tortilla
  6. Roll burrito up, and toast on the stove

Ingredients:

  • 300g Chicken breast
  • 1tsp Olive oil
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 1/2tsp Pepper
  • 1/2tsp Chili powder
  • 1/2tsp Cumin
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4cup Greek yogurt
  • 1tbsp Hot sauce
  • 2 large tortillas
  • 1/4cup Low fat cheese
