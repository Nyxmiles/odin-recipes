Egg Bites

Preparation:

  1. Blend cottage cheese, egg beaters, seasoning
  2. Pour into muffin tray
  3. Top with basil,spinach and canadian bacon
  4. Bake for 15-20 @ 375F

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2cup Egg beaters
  • 8oz Cottage cheese
  • 1/2cup Reduced fat cheddar
  • 4 Slices canadian bacon
  • Spinach
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper
