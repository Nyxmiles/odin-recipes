Lava Cake

Preparation:

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Pour mixture into a mug, submerging a chocolate piece in the center
  3. Microwave for 25-40s

Ingredients:

  • 15g Oat flour
  • 5g Cocoa powder
  • 10g Protein powder
  • 1/2tsp baking powder
  • 35ml almond milk
  • 10g chocolate
